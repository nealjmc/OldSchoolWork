Types

There are many different types of keyboards in this world. Some of them are big while others are small. Most keyboards have either a rubber dome, or a membrane type connection to the main PCB. These are cheap and easy to make for the normal consumer keyboard.

Mechanical Keyboards have physical switches connecting to the main PCB on the board. This means that a linier switch is pressed down on the board, connecting to the main PCB, finishing a circuit and sending a keystroke to the computer.

cN - centinewton. 1cN = One hundrenth of a newton. One newton = 1kg/(meter/second squared)
